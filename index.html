This is a super minimal assembler for the MIPS processor developed by the project goup FPGA 2020 at Uni Bonn.

This Processor unconditionally executes one instruction after every jump and three after every beq.

Supported Instructions are: add, sub, and, or, nor, lw, sw, addi, beq, j, and break.

li is implemented as pseudo instruction.

Comments can be at the end of the line, beginning with "#" or ";".
